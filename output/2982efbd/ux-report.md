**⚠️ Issues:**
- 🟡 **MAJOR:** No social proof visible above the fold (reviews, ratings, user count, testimonials)

- 🟡 **MAJOR:** Vague value proposition lacks specificity - 'free up a ton of space' doesn't quantify benefits

- ⚪ **MINOR:** The guarantee badge showing '30' is partially cut off and unclear
  - *Fix:* Make the money-back guarantee badge more prominent and fully visible
- ⚪ **MINOR:** Cookie consent banner covers potential content at the bottom of the viewport
  - *Fix:* Use a less intrusive cookie banner design or move to a corner toast notification

**🚧 Friction Points:**
- Users may hesitate without seeing pricing information before clicking 'Buy Now'
- No indication of what happens after the 7-day trial (auto-charge? reminder?)
- Lack of feature breakdown may leave users unclear on what exactly the tool does

**⚡ Quick Wins:**
