### Step 1: Landing Page 🟡 7/10

**Summary:** Clean, visually appealing landing page with clear value proposition and prominent CTAs. The purple/pink gradient creates strong brand identity, but the page lacks social proof above the fold and some trust signals that could improve conversion rates.

**✅ What works well:**
- Strong visual hierarchy with clear product icon and headline
- Dual CTA strategy (Try Free + Buy Now) caters to different user intents
- 30-day money back guarantee reduces purchase anxiety
- Clean navigation with minimal distractions (only FAQ and Blog links)
- Value proposition clearly states benefits: free up space, block malware, reduce lag
- Consistent green CTA color creates clear action points

**⚠️ Issues:**
- 🟡 **MAJOR:** No social proof visible above the fold - no user counts, ratings, or testimonials
  - *Fix:* Add trust indicators like '10M+ downloads', App Store rating, or brief testimonials near the CTAs
- 🟡 **MAJOR:** Cookie consent banner partially obscures page content and lacks clear accept/reject buttons
  - *Fix:* Redesign cookie banner with prominent Accept/Reject buttons and ensure it doesn't cover important content
- ⚪ **MINOR:** The '30' in the money-back guarantee appears cut off or styled inconsistently
  - *Fix:* Ensure the '30-day money back guarantee' text is fully visible and styled consistently
- ⚪ **MINOR:** No indication of pricing visible, users must click to discover cost
  - *Fix:* Consider showing starting price or 'From $X/year' near Buy Now button to set expectations

**🚧 Friction Points:**
- Users don't know the price before clicking Buy Now - could cause drop-off
- No clear indication of what 'Try 7 Days Free' requires (credit card? email only?)
- Cookie banner creates visual noise and requires additional user action
- No visible system requirements - Mac users may wonder about compatibility with their OS version

**⚡ Quick Wins:**
