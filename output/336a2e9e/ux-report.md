## Step-by-Step Analysis

### Step 1: Landing Page 🟡 7/10

**Summary:** CleanMyMac's landing page has a clean, visually appealing design with clear value proposition and prominent CTAs. However, the page lacks social proof above the fold and the cookie consent banner creates unnecessary friction at the bottom of the viewport.

**✅ What works well:**
- Strong visual hierarchy with prominent app icon and clear headline
- Dual CTA strategy ('Try 7 Days Free' and 'Buy Now') caters to different user intents
- 30-day money back guarantee reduces purchase anxiety
- Value proposition clearly communicated: free up space, block malware, reduce lag
- Consistent brand colors (pink/magenta) create visual cohesion
- Header includes both trial and purchase options for easy access

**⚠️ Issues:**
- 🟡 **MAJOR:** No social proof visible above the fold - no ratings, reviews, download counts, or testimonials
  - *Fix:* Add trust indicators like '10M+ downloads', App Store rating, or brief testimonial near the CTAs
- 🟡 **MAJOR:** Cookie consent banner obscures bottom content and creates visual clutter
  - *Fix:* Use a less intrusive cookie notice design or move to a corner toast notification
- ⚪ **MINOR:** The tagline 'A Mac cleaner? A Mac saver.' is clever but doesn't immediately communicate concrete benefits
  - *Fix:* Consider A/B testing with more benefit-driven headline like 'Clean up X GB of junk in minutes'
- ⚪ **MINOR:** No indication of pricing or what happens after the 7-day trial ends
  - *Fix:* Add brief pricing context like 'Plans from $X/year' to set expectations

**🚧 Friction Points:**
- Users may hesitate without knowing the price before clicking 'Buy Now'
- No system requirements visible - users unsure if compatible with their Mac/macOS version
- Cookie banner requires interaction before fully engaging with page
- Limited navigation options (only FAQ and Blog) may leave users wanting more information

**⚡ Quick Wins:**
- Add star rating and review count from App Store near the CTA buttons
- Include a small 'Works with macOS Sonoma' badge for compatibility assurance
- Add 'No credit card required' under the free trial button to reduce friction
- Replace intrusive cookie banner with a smaller, dismissible corner notification
