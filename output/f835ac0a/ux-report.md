**Summary:** Clean, visually appealing landing page with clear value proposition and strong CTAs. The design effectively communicates the product's purpose, but lacks some trust signals and could improve information hierarchy for better conversion optimization.

**✅ What works well:**
- Strong visual hierarchy with prominent app icon and clear headline
- Dual CTA strategy ('Try 7 Days Free' and 'Buy Now') caters to different user intents
- Trust indicators at bottom (Apple notarization, 29M downloads, 4.9 rating, 17 years expertise, IF Design Award)
- Money-back guarantee prominently displayed reduces purchase anxiety
- Consistent brand colors (pink/purple gradient) create memorable visual identity
- Concise value proposition covers key benefits: space, malware, performance

**⚠️ Issues:**
- 🟡 **MAJOR:** No pricing information visible on landing page
  - *Fix:* Add pricing transparency near the 'Buy Now' button or include a pricing tier preview to reduce friction before users commit to exploring further
- 🟡 **MAJOR:** No product demonstration or feature preview showing actual functionality
  - *Fix:* Add a hero video, animated GIF, or interactive demo showing the scanning/cleaning process to build confidence in the product
- ⚪ **MINOR:** The '30-day money back guarantee' badge shows '30' in a hard-to-read stylized format
  - *Fix:* Make the '30' more legible or spell out 'Thirty' for better readability
- ⚪ **MINOR:** Navigation is minimal with only FAQ and Blog links
  - *Fix:* Add links to Features, Pricing, and Support to help users who need more information before converting

**🚧 Friction Points:**
- Users don't know what the free trial includes or requires (credit card?)
- No visible system requirements - users unsure if it works with their macOS version
- Lack of social proof beyond stats (no testimonials or real user quotes visible)
- No comparison with alternatives or clear differentiators from competitors

**⚡ Quick Wins:**
