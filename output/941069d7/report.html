Step 1: Landing Page

7/10

CleanMyMac's landing page features strong visual branding with a clear value proposition and prominent CTAs. The page effectively communicates core benefits but lacks social proof above the fold and has some minor usability concerns with the cookie banner placement.

✅ What works

  • Clear, benefit-driven headline that immediately communicates value ('A Mac cleaner? A Mac saver.')
  • Strong visual hierarchy with prominent app icon creating brand recognition
  • Dual CTA strategy with 'Try 7 Days Free' (primary/green) and 'Buy Now' (secondary/outlined) reduces commitment anxiety
  • 30-day money back guarantee displayed near CTAs builds trust
  • Clean, uncluttered design with good use of whitespace
  • Consistent branding with pink/purple gradient creating memorable aesthetic

⚠️ Issues

MAJOR: No social proof visible above the fold - missing user counts, ratings, or testimonials
→ Add App Store rating, number of downloads, or brief testimonial quote near the CTAs
MAJOR: Cookie consent banner obscures bottom content and creates visual clutter
→ Use a less intrusive cookie banner design, possibly a smaller floating element or slide-in from corner
MINOR: Navigation is minimal with only FAQ and Blog - no clear path to learn more about features
→ Add 'Features' or 'How it works' link in navigation for users who need more information before converting
MINOR: The '30' in the money-back guarantee appears as a separate element/badge that may not be immediately clear
→ Display '30-day money back guarantee' as continuous text for better readability

⚡ Quick Wins

  • Add App Store rating badge (e.g., '4.8 stars from 50,000+ reviews') near CTAs
  • Include 'As featured in' press logo strip below the fold
  • Add micro-copy under 'Try 7 Days Free' clarifying 'No credit card required' if applicable
  • Make the 30-day guarantee more prominent with a small icon/checkmark
